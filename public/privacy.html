TRUCKERS NAV BY TUE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: We collect your device's GPS coordinates to provide turn-by-turn navigation, show nearby truck stops, display traffic information, and power hazard alerts. Location data is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers beyond the active session.

Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name for authentication purposes.

Community Reports: When you submit community reports (parking availability, fuel prices, road hazards, weigh station status), we store the report content, approximate location, and your chosen display name.

ELD Logs: Electronic Logging Device data (driving status, hours) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly export it.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymized usage analytics (routes searched, features used) to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide GPS navigation and truck-specific routing
  • Display real-time traffic, weather, and hazard alerts
  • Power the community data layer (shared reports)
  • Generate parking availability predictions
  • Improve app performance and features

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • HERE Technologies — Routing, traffic data, and geocoding
  • Mapbox — Map tile rendering
  • Google Maps Platform — Places search and geocoding
  • Google Gemini AI — Traffic incident analysis (anonymized)
  • Firebase — Authentication and user profiles

4. Data Storage & Security

HUD layout preferences, ELD logs, and cached map tiles are stored locally on your device. Community reports are stored on our servers. We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Community reports you submit are visible to other app users. Location data is shared with third-party mapping services only as needed to provide navigation functionality.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account and associated data at any time through the app's Settings. ELD logs can be exported and deleted locally. Community reports can be managed through the Community tab.

7. Children's Privacy

TRUCKERS NAV is designed for professional truck drivers and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@truckersnav.com
