# TRUCKERS NAV By TUE - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 25, 2026

## Introduction

TRUCKERS NAV By TUE ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile navigation application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **GPS Location:** We collect real-time location data to provide turn-by-turn navigation, route planning, and nearby POI information.
- **Background Location:** With your permission, we collect location data in the background to continue navigation while the app is not actively in use.
- **Usage:** Location data is used solely for navigation purposes and is not shared with third parties for advertising.

### Device Information
- Device model and operating system version
- Network connectivity status
- App usage statistics and crash reports

### User Preferences
- Saved routes and favorite locations
- Navigation settings and preferences
- ELD (Electronic Logging Device) status preferences
- Truck profile information (height, weight, axle count)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide accurate turn-by-turn navigation
- Display nearby truck stops, rest areas, and points of interest
- Calculate optimal routes based on truck restrictions
- Show real-time traffic and road conditions
- Provide predictive parking availability
- Send navigation alerts and warnings

## Data Storage and Security

- Location data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers
- User preferences are stored locally on your device
- Route history is stored locally and can be deleted at any time
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission

## Third-Party Services

This app integrates with the following third-party services:
- **HERE Maps API:** For mapping, routing, and POI data
- **OpenStreetMap:** For traffic infrastructure and supplemental POI data
- **Firebase:** For authentication and crash reporting (if enabled)

These services have their own privacy policies governing data usage.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your stored data
- Delete your account and data
- Opt-out of location tracking (note: this will disable navigation features)
- Control background location access through device settings

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website.

## Contact Us

- Email: privacy@truckersnav.com

## Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data:
1. Open the app
2. Go to Settings → Privacy
3. Select "Delete My Data"
4. Or email us at privacy@truckersnav.com
